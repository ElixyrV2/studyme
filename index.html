Assignment 1.2: Research Plan

Background

In our country at the moment there are no companies that are engaged in sending students abroad. There are no companies that prepare students, help them write entrance essays, prepare them for exams, and prepare documents. We want to fix that. We want to raise Kazakh specialists.

Goals

• To prepare people for admission and education abroad.
• To give people the opportunity to see the world.
• To give people the opportunity to get a better education.
• To save money on studies.

Research Questions

What is the problem to be solved?
The problem of not being able to go abroad.
What is your solution to the problem?
A company that will help people enroll abroad.
Who cares about this problem and why?
Schoolchildren, students, their parents.

Research Methods

• Surveys or questionnaires.
• Experiments/analysis.
• Focus Groups.
• Interviews.

Target Audience

Who is our user?
• Location: Kazakhstan
• Age: 15-30
• Salary: ~700$ / mo.
